WELCOME...

Face2Face are a group of Christian professional musicians who refresh audiences everywhere with a unique mixture of music, ranging from classical, rock, jazz, pop, ballads, opera and Christian songs.

Our aim is to perform a range of music to the highest professional standards conveying the love of God in a contemporary way.

OUR BELIEF

Our belief is that a single message can begin to make a huge difference in someone's life, causing them to come 'face to face' with the love of God, shown in his dear son, the Lord Jesus Christ. The ministry of Face2Face is both national and international.
