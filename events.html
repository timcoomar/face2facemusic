OUR EVENTS

As a Christian music group, we have been invited to perform our blend of music throughout the UK and Russia.Our performances have taken us into concert halls, prisons, orphanages, schools, church halls, shopping centres and jazz cafes, just to name a few.

If you are interested in hosting a concert then please call Judith on 020 8449 7324 to ask for further details.
